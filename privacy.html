Privacy & data use

Effective July 2026

Tax Grid Consultants Limited respects the privacy of people who contact the firm. This notice explains how information may be received and used through the website, email, telephone, WhatsApp and professional-service enquiries.

Information we may receive

This may include a person's name, contact details, company information and the description of the accounting, tax, payroll or business-support matter raised.

How information is used

Information may be used to understand and respond to an enquiry, prepare proposals and onboarding requirements, deliver agreed services, meet legal and regulatory obligations, and maintain appropriate business and client records. Personal information is not sold to advertisers.

Website enquiry form

The website enquiry form prepares a message on the visitor's device and opens either WhatsApp or the visitor's email application. The website itself does not store the information typed into the form.

Retention and security

Information received by Tax Grid Consultants is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the enquiry, professional engagement, legal obligations and appropriate business records. Reasonable measures are used to protect information under the firm's control, although no electronic communication method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your enquiries

Questions about personal information or this notice may be sent to info@taxgridconsultants.com.
