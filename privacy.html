Privacy & data use

Tax Grid Consultants Limited respects the privacy of people who contact the firm. Information submitted through email, telephone or website enquiry channels is used to understand the enquiry, respond to the person and provide requested professional services.

Information we may receive

This may include a person's name, contact details, company information and the description of the accounting, tax, payroll or business-support matter raised.

How information is used

Information is used for communication, client onboarding, professional service delivery, compliance administration and maintaining appropriate business records. Information is not sold to advertisers.
