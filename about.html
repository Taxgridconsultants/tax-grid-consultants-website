THE TGC APPROACH

Modern systems are valuable. Professional responsibility remains essential.

TGC combines accounting, tax, payroll, company and advisory support through a clear working relationship. Technology is used to improve speed, organisation and visibility—but never as a substitute for judgement, review and communication.

The firm is based in Mauritius and designed to collaborate with businesses, founders and stakeholders locally and internationally.
